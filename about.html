Who We Are

Practical digital solutions with a clean, professional edge.

RSG TECH is a modern technology solutions brand created by Abhiroop Goel, focused on building websites, custom software tools, and automation systems for businesses, brands, teams, organizations, and professionals.

The mission is to create practical digital solutions that look professional, work smoothly, and make everyday tasks easier. From responsive websites to inventory systems, dashboards, PowerApps applications, SharePoint workflows, and Microsoft 365 automation, RSG TECH builds tools around real workflows.

With a background in computer science, web development, IT support, and business process automation, RSG TECH combines technical skill with practical problem solving. Every project is built with clean design, clear communication, and long-term usability in mind.
